# Printable Checklist (Sample)

## Immediate containment
- [ ] Reset passwords for affected users.
- [ ] Revoke active sessions for email/admin accounts.
- [ ] Enforce MFA on critical accounts.
- [ ] Check mailbox forwarding and suspicious inbox rules.
- [ ] Notify staff with verified internal guidance.

## First 7 days
- [ ] Confirm endpoint patch status on all business devices.
- [ ] Review remote access paths (VPN/RDP/admin portals).
- [ ] Validate backup integrity and restore process.
- [ ] Restrict third-party/vendor access to least privilege.
- [ ] Assign owner and due date for each mitigation action.

## Ongoing hardening
- [ ] Schedule monthly access and MFA coverage review.
- [ ] Track recurring phishing training participation.
- [ ] Re-run readiness brief after major business/system changes.

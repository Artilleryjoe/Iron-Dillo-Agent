# v1 Use Cases: Rural Small Business Security Brief Generator

## Primary persona

**Operations owner / office manager** at a small or rural business that needs a
clear cybersecurity action plan without requiring deep security expertise.

## Secondary persona

**Local IT partner / MSP advisor** who needs a repeatable intake and output
format for multiple clients.

## Scenario catalog (v1)

### 1) Phishing concern
- **Trigger:** suspicious messages, reported credential prompt, or unusual login.
- **Goal:** contain account risk and reduce immediate compromise potential.
- **Typical outputs:** account lock/reset sequence, mailbox review checklist,
  employee communication steps.

### 2) Weak passwords or missing MFA
- **Trigger:** reused passwords, no MFA on critical systems.
- **Goal:** reduce account takeover risk.
- **Typical outputs:** prioritized MFA rollout, password policy baseline,
  critical account hardening list.

### 3) Exposed website or remote access issue
- **Trigger:** public admin panel, risky RDP/VPN posture, misconfigured edge service.
- **Goal:** reduce internet-facing attack surface.
- **Typical outputs:** emergency exposure mitigation, access path inventory,
  short-term hardening plan.

### 4) Device loss
- **Trigger:** lost/stolen laptop or phone with business data access.
- **Goal:** contain identity and data exposure quickly.
- **Typical outputs:** remote revoke/wipe steps, account/session invalidation,
  notification and audit trail checklist.

### 5) Vendor/software trust concern
- **Trigger:** supplier breach news, suspicious update behavior, unclear vendor controls.
- **Goal:** assess third-party impact and immediate compensating controls.
- **Typical outputs:** vendor risk triage notes, access scoping actions,
  dependency monitoring checklist.

### 6) General readiness check
- **Trigger:** no active incident; business wants baseline posture assessment.
- **Goal:** establish practical first-stage cybersecurity roadmap.
- **Typical outputs:** top control gaps, 7-day starter plan,
  longer-term maturity actions.
